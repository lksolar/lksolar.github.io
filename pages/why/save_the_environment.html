Save the Environment!

Saving the planet by using freely available and endless Solar energy is a no brainer. Based on “standard” charging rates from the biggest provider – Electric Ireland – the average household usage in Ireland is 4200 kWh electricity. The SEAI calculates that a single kWh of electricity leads to the emission of 635 grammes of CO2. ( https://www.seai.ie/publications/Energy-Related-Emissions-in-Ireland-2016-report.pdf ) At these rates typical 20m2 PV system generating 2600 kWh over the year will typically save one and a quarter tonnes of CO2. Solar, like any energy source, has a carbon footprint. But this is very small, only about fifty grammes of CO2 per kWh or 5% of the typical value for the main fossil fuels. You save money and the planet is cleaner. Simple.
